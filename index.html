THIRST FOR ANNIHILATION

Meme Koin | Kaspa | Trading on Chainge & KSPRBOT


The future presents itself to us more and more in the pure form of acceleration. This form practically engulfs us at the frontiers of economics, technology, and culture. The memecoin is a simplified expression of the coalescence of these three manifestations of the primacy of acceleration. Rarely, however, does a memecoin wholeheartedly embrace this coalescence of the fast, tending on the contrary to domesticated imagery and repetitive sloganeering. Rarely does a memecoin project deploy its energies with a techno-optimist vision of the space within which it creates, as per Marc Andreesen’s “Techno-Optimist Manifesto.”

Enter $FAST coin: a memecoin on the Kaspa blockchain originally spawned through an affinity with the accelerationist philosophy of Nick Land – a philosophy whose history continues to ooze memetic artifacts, but which is essentially based in the future.

In the words of Land, Capital is machinic (non-instrumental) globalization-miniaturization scaling dilation: an automatizing nihilist vortex, neutralizing all values through commensuration to digitized commerce (Meltdown), and we say, why not finally put a tokenomic face to the ur-plasticity that is capital and Capitalism? The ‘Why?’ of FAST coin was already decided wherein the market first ever computed a transaction. Because monetary economic acceleration needs a mascot, it needs a clear sign. A cryptocurrency signifier of everything fast. A tokenized figurehead of the future arriving.

With the emergence of the KRC20 protocol, the Kaspa blockchain – currently boasting a single second block interval but promising even higher speeds in the future – was a natural choice for $FAST coin. If Satoshi Nakamoto’s ‘Bitcoin P2P e-cash paper’ obscurely envisaged culture to come, then Kaspa accelerates that culture to its terminal limit, seeking to operate as fast as internet speed allows.

The $FAST token was one of twenty to be created June 30, 2024 on Kaspa when the KRC-20 protocol first went live, alongside $SILVER, $KEKE, $NACHO, and $GHOAD.

The FAST team consists of eleven committed artists, philosophers, and developers. We are committed to the Kaspa technology and ethos. We are also naturally inclined to produce content and engage with the community according to our own idiosyncratic proclivities and preferences – plugged in, but driven to act upon the network in turn, to feed the glitches through which we escape the rusty old paradigms and embrace the accelerant future.

With non-fungible token support coming to the Kaspa blockchain through the KRC-721 protocol, the FAST team will have the opportunity to leverage both the abilities and proclivities of the team, as well as the copious work that has already gone into content creation. This proliferation and crystallization of the FAST vision in the form of generative NFTs is a natural development in the evolution of FAST’s snail logo and FAST content more broadly. The FAST team includes artists working intensively with digital production methods, including LLMs as well as generative NFT software, but also with more traditional media. The NFTs created by FAST will include a mixture of layering and traits with AI automation and randomization. The latter processes are not at all gratuitous or indiscriminate. They are rather highly selective, the product of being terminally online and discovering more and more source content fit for inclusion in various models trained according to decisive intentional tastes and criteria, stimulated also by the unconstrained nature of the results.

Finally, in addition to the token and the NFTs, the volute entity known as FAST is in the early phases of developing a DAO. The FAST DAO will begin acquiring both NFTs and physical fine art as part of its strategy to become a real-world asset (RWA) token. The works in the DAO's collection will serve as marketing tools for the token, showcased through physical exhibitions around the world as well as online. The DAO's current collection includes pieces by Gunter Brus, Antonius Hoeckelmann, and Eric Schmid.

Tokenization is a process that allows real-world assets (RWA) to be converted into digital tokens. FAST is leading the way in providing a secure and efficient solution for tokenizing RWAs as this industry continues to grow. Art is just one of these assets that can be tokenized. By tokenizing art, investors can own a fraction of the artwork without having to purchase the entire piece. This allows for greater access to valuable artworks and democratizes the art market.
