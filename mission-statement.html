The decision to pre-mint and thus take 100% control of the supply in the beginning (as well as 100% responsibility for its distribution) was based on multiple factors:

1) the desire to reward $FAST followers and early participants in the KRC-20 ecosystem with a largescale airdrop;

2) concerns over sophisticated actors gaming the mint and taking control of the supply; and

3) the realization that there would be a significant net benefit to cheaply pre-minting the tokens ourselves, thereby avoiding the high transaction fees incurred during the launch of KRC-20 due to network congestion as well as other risks to ourselves and the community.

 

Now, we plan to airdrop the remaining 40% of $FAST tokens set aside for the purpose. If you have not already done so, follow our instructions on Twitter (quote tweet with a Kaspa address followed by the snail emoji) and you will be added to the airdrop list. For those wanting to know more:

 

$FAST is an accelerationist memecoin, and yet we have chosen to distribute our tokens through successive rounds of airdrops, a process which is by nature slow and halting.

Rather than pulling addresses haphazardly, we reward people who demonstrate active involvement with the $FAST project and the KRC-20 ecosystem more broadly. Meanwhile, we are able to contribute our own memetic vision to the latter. We have been there for the drama, we have willfully made ourselves a part of it, we relish its energy and create from it.
